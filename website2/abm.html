Accountancy, Business and Management

The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Strand at LSPU LB equips students with essential business and financial skills, making it the perfect choice for aspiring entrepreneurs, accountants, and corporate leaders. This strand focuses on business management, marketing, accounting, and finance, providing hands-on experience in real-world business scenarios. With the growing demand for skilled professionals in the business world, ABM opens doors to endless career opportunities. Take the first step toward success by enrolling in the ABM Strand at LSPU LB!

Activities

ABM students focus on business management, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship. They develop skills in leadership, financial analysis, and strategic planning, preparing for careers in business, accounting, sales, and corporate management. ABM students learn how to run businesses, manage resources, and make informed financial decisions for success in the business world.
