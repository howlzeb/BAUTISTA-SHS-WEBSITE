Agri-Fisheries

pThe Agrifisheries Strand at LSPU LB offers students a hands-on learning experience in agriculture, fisheries, and agribusiness. This strand focuses on sustainable farming techniques, aquaculture, and modern agricultural technologies to equip students with the skills needed in the agricultural sector. With the increasing importance of food security and environmental sustainability, Agrifisheries graduates play a vital role in shaping the future of food production. Be part of a thriving industry and build a sustainable future with the Agrifisheries Strand at LSPU LB!

Activities

Agrifisheries students learn about modern farming, aquaculture, and agribusiness. They study sustainable agriculture, fishery production, and food security while gaining hands-on experience in cultivating crops, raising livestock, and managing fisheries. This strand prepares students for careers in agribusiness, farm management, and environmental sustainability, contributing to food production and rural development.
